About Koinonia Haven

Koinonia Haven is a sober living home for men who are ready to take the next step after treatment, detox, incarceration, or a difficult season of life. Our focus is simple: provide a safe, stable, and respectful home environment where residents can practice sober living one day at a time.

We are not a treatment center or medical facility. Instead, we offer a structured, recovery-friendly home where men can rebuild routines, work on personal goals, and learn how to live independently while staying accountable. Residents are encouraged to stay connected to outside supports such as meetings, sponsors, mentors, or counselors, while we provide the consistent housing and day-to-day structure they need to succeed.

Located in Deer Park, TX, Koinonia Haven is intentionally small and community-oriented. This allows us to maintain a calm atmosphere, clear expectations, and a culture of mutual respect so that everyone in the house can focus on staying sober and moving forward.

What We Offer

Our home is designed to support men who are serious about long-term change. We combine a peaceful physical environment with structure, house expectations, and peer support so residents are not doing recovery alone.

Comfortable, Stable Housing

Residents live in a clean, shared home setting that feels like a real house—not an institution. The focus is on safety, comfort, and consistency.

  • Fully furnished common areas and bedrooms
  • All utilities included in one flat program fee
  • Kitchen access for preparing personal meals
  • Wi-Fi available throughout the home

Daily Structure & Expectations

Structure is a key part of recovery. We keep the environment orderly and predictable so residents can focus on rebuilding their lives.

  • House rules that support sobriety and respect
  • Curfew and guest policies for everyone’s safety
  • Chore rotations to keep the home clean and shared
  • Encouragement to pursue work, meetings, or education

Community & Accountability

Residents live alongside others who understand what it means to start over. No one has to do it alone.

  • Recovery-minded peer environment
  • Built-in accountability through shared living
  • Drug and alcohol–free home with clear consequences
  • Supportive atmosphere focused on forward progress

Why Choose Koinonia Haven?

Choosing the right sober living home matters. Koinonia Haven is built for men who want more than a place to sleep—they want a place to grow, stabilize, and prepare for what’s next.

✓
Recovery-focused environment. Everyone in the home is expected to remain sober, respect the rules, and stay committed to positive change.
✓
Clear rules and consistent follow-through. Expectations are laid out from the beginning so residents understand what is required and what will not be tolerated.
✓
Home-like setting, not a facility. The atmosphere is warm and residential, helping residents feel safe and respected while they adjust to sober living.
✓
Ideal step-down after higher levels of care. Koinonia Haven works well as a transition after detox, residential treatment, jail, or unstable housing, when someone needs structure but also independence.
✓
Consistent communication with partners (when requested). With proper releases, we can coordinate with case managers, probation, courts, or treatment programs to support continuity of housing and expectations.
✓
Emphasis on dignity and respect. Residents are treated as adults who are responsible for their choices, while also being encouraged and supported in day-to-day life.
✓
Encouragement toward healthy routines. We promote work, meetings, faith practices, exercise, and other positive habits that support long-term stability.
✓
Convenient Deer Park location. The home is situated near stores, potential job opportunities, and main roads, making it easier for residents to get to work, appointments, and meetings.
✓
Accountability with a fresh start. Koinonia Haven blends grace and structure—residents get a real opportunity to start fresh, with clear boundaries that protect the safety of the house.
